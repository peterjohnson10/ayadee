DAOs & Cooperative Governance

Bridging 180 years of cooperative history with decentralized coordination to design the next generation of organizations.

Target Audience

Web3 founders, institutional designers, and cooperative leaders looking to leverage Mondragon-style governance through blockchain protocols.

Syllabus

Module 1 The Mondragon Legacy

Analyzing lessons from the world's largest worker-owned cooperative. Identifying core principles that have sustained resilience for decades.

Module 2 Decentralized Mechanics

Voting models, tokenomics, and the "one person, one vote" vs. "one token, one vote" debate. Practical DAO tooling for institutional coordination.

Module 3 Algorithmic Trust & Smart Contracts

How code automates coordination. Designing smart contracts that mirror real-world cooperative values and democratic participation.

Module 4 Hybrid Institutional Design

Integrating DAOs into traditional legal systems. Navigating the regulatory and operational hurdles of decentralized entities in global markets.
