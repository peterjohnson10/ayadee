The Perspective

Where Organizational Research Meets Frontier AI

As a Finance Fellow with Scale AI’s Human Frontier Collective, I work with ML research teams shaping how frontier AI models understand financial systems. This practical work complements my PhD research at the University of the Basque Country, where I explore how decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) and 180 years of cooperative history inform innovation.

A former U.S. Foreign Service Officer, I have spent my career coordinating global coalitions and managing development programs. Through the Ayadee Foundation and Blue Shift Labs, I design strategies for emerging technologies for multilateral organizations like the FAO, UNDP, and the European Commission.
