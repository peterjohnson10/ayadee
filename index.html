Bridging Research & Global Strategy

As a Finance Fellow with Scale AI’s Human Frontier Collective, I work with ML research teams on how frontier models understand financial systems. This practical work informs my PhD research on Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) and cooperative governance at the University of the Basque Country.

A former U.S. Foreign Service Officer, I have managed global coalitions and development programs. Through the Ayadee Foundation, I lead a team designing strategies for emerging technologies for multilateral organizations like the FAO, UNDP, and the European Commission.

Knowledge Production

Architects of the Future Fellowship

A selective program for emerging leaders working at the intersection of technology, governance, and global impact. The fellowship emphasizes knowledge production, applied research, and think tank–style contributions.

Program Structure & Contribution

6-month, part-time fellowship (8–12 hours/week), remote-first. Fellows contribute directly to research, program development, and real-world advisory projects in AI governance and institutional transformation.

Applied Research Focus
  • AI Governance: Policy frameworks and risk mitigation for frontier models.
  • Strategic Foresight: Building scenario models for digital ecosystems.
  • Digital Economy: Researching fintech policy and digital identity systems.
  • Institutional Design: Investigating how DAOs and cooperatives enable participatory oversight of emerging tech.
Who Should Apply

Candidates demonstrating strong analytical capability and a background in policy, entrepreneurship, or international development.
