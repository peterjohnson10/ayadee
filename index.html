Strategic Foresight, Leadership & Institutional Governance

The Ayadee Foundation works at the intersection of strategic foresight, leadership development, and institutional governance. Our programs equip senior leaders and organizations to navigate systemic change driven by frontier technology, shifting geopolitical conditions, and the emergence of new organizational forms.

Our initiatives are led by Peter Johnson, a strategic foresight consultant, educator, and former U.S. Foreign Service Officer who has advised multilateral organizations including the European Commission, UNDP, and FAO across 34+ countries. Johnson teaches executive education at Humboldt University Berlin, serves as AI Expert for the EU Global Technical Assistance Facility, and holds a Finance Fellowship with Scale AI’s Human Frontier Collective. His doctoral research at the University of the Basque Country examines how cooperative and decentralized governance structures shape institutional resilience.

Architects of the Future Fellowship

A selective program for emerging leaders working at the intersection of technology, governance, and global impact. The fellowship emphasizes knowledge production, applied research, and think tank–style contributions.

Program Structure

6-month, part-time fellowship (8–12 hours/week), remote-first. Fellows contribute directly to research, program development, and real-world advisory projects in AI governance.

Who Should Apply

Candidates demonstrating strong analytical capability and a background in policy, entrepreneurship, or international development. We seek those committed to using technology for global impact.

Applied Research Focus

  • AI Governance: Policy frameworks and risk mitigation for frontier models.
  • Strategic Foresight: Building scenario models for digital ecosystems.
  • Digital Economy: Researching fintech policy and digital identity systems.
  • Institutional Design: Investigating how DAOs enable participatory oversight.
