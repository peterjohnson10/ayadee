Bridging Research & Global Strategy

The Ayadee Foundation operates at the intersection of machine learning research and international policy. Our work is driven by a deep understanding of how frontier AI models intersect with financial systems and institutional governance.

Our initiatives are led by Peter Johnson, a Finance Fellow with Scale AI and PhD researcher in Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs). A former U.S. Foreign Service Officer, Johnson has executed strategic research and advisory mandates for major multilateral organizations, including the FAO, UNDP, and the European Commission.
