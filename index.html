Bridging Research & Global Strategy

As a Finance Fellow with Scale AI, I work with ML research teams on how frontier models understand financial systems. This practical work informs my PhD research on DAOs and cooperative governance at the University of the Basque Country.

A former U.S. Foreign Service Officer, I led global coalitions and development programs. Through the Ayadee Foundation, I lead a team designing strategies for emerging technologies for multilateral organizations like the FAO, UNDP, and the European Commission.
