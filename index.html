Leadership & Strategy

The Human Frontier

Chief Strategic Foresight Officer Peter Johnson directs the Foundation’s mission to bridge the gap between emerging technology and institutional resilience. Peter brings a sophisticated background in global policy as a former U.S. Foreign Service Officer and currently serves as a Finance Fellow with Scale AI, focusing on the systemic implications of frontier AI models. His academic work explores the evolution of DAOs and Cooperative Governance, providing a historical and technical roadmap for decentralized coordination.

The Foundation operates as a lean, high-impact node, drawing on a wide-ranging collective of partners and subject-matter experts. Through Blue Shift Labs by Ayadee, the Foundation provides specialized advisory and research, leveraging a global network that spans multilateral organizations, machine learning laboratories, and social innovation hubs.
