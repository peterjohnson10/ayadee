Institutional Perspective

Bridging Research & Global Strategy

The Ayadee Foundation operates at the intersection of machine learning research and international policy. Our work is driven by a deep understanding of how frontier AI models intersect with financial systems and institutional governance.

Our initiatives are led by Peter Johnson, a Finance Fellow with Scale AI and PhD researcher in Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs). A former U.S. Foreign Service Officer, Johnson brings a career of managing global coalitions for the FAO, UNDP, and the European Commission to the Foundation's strategic mission.

Knowledge Production

Architects of the Future Fellowship

A selective program for emerging leaders working at the intersection of technology, governance, and global impact. The fellowship emphasizes knowledge production, applied research, and think tank–style contributions.

Program Structure

6-month, part-time fellowship (8–12 hours/week), remote-first. Fellows contribute directly to research, program development, and real-world advisory projects in AI governance.

Applied Research Focus

  • AI Governance: Policy frameworks and risk mitigation for frontier models.
  • Strategic Foresight: Building scenario models for digital ecosystems.
  • Digital Economy: Researching fintech policy and digital identity systems.
  • Institutional Design: How DAOs enable participatory oversight.
